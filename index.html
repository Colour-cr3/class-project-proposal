About

Hi, I’m Patrick.

I make images that keep their breath. I lean on natural light, minimal color grading, and crisp composition. When I’m not shooting, I’m hiking, tinkering with web projects, and drinking coffee I pretend is better than it is.

Tools: Canon EOS R6 Mark II • DJI Osmo Action 5 Pro • Canon RF 24–105mm f/4L • Tamron EF 70–200mm f/2.8 G2 • Adobe Lightroom
